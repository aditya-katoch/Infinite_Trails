The Best of Shimla & Manali 05 Nights & 06 Days by Cab (Ex-Delhi)

DAY 1

Delhi- Shimla (350 Km/8-9 hrs)

▼
  • On arrival at Delhi Airport/ Railway Station you will be met, assisted and drive to Shimla.It will take around 8-9 hrs to reach Shimla by surface from Delhi. it is spectacularly set at 2,154 metres with the snow-capped Himalaya as a backdrop and with beautiful promenades, graceful colonial buildings, wooded ravines and evergreen forests. On arrival check in to hotel.
  • Overnight Stay at Shimla.
DAY 2

Shimla-Kufri& City Tour

▼
  • Morning post breakfast visit for excursion to the green valleys of Fagu and Kufri. {Optional: Visit Kufri Adventure resort (Guest can enjoy various rides & activities at their own cost)}. Explore the Kufri Winter Sports Capital, Indira Bungalow and Himalayan Zoo.
  • Post lunch drive back to town to visit Indian Institute of Advanced Study (formerly known as Vice Regal Lodge, closed on Monday), The Christ Church, Jakhu Temple (Via rope Way), Gaiety Theatre, Scandal Point, The Mall Road and The Ridge.
  • Overnight stay at Shimla.
DAY 3

Shimla - Manali (270 Km/8-9 hrs)

▼
  • Morning drive from Shimla to Manali. It’s an 8-9 hour long journey. En-route pass through Pandoh Dam, Beas River, Sutlej River, Beas Sutlej Link Project, Kullu Valley. On arrival you will move into the hotel in Manali and retire for the night.
  • Overnight stay at Manali.
DAY 4

Manali – Solang valley- Manali

▼
  • Morning at leisure. Post breakfast proceed for day excursion to Solang Valley- A splendid valley between Solang village and Beas Kund which offers the view of glaciers and snowcapped mountains and peaks which has fine ski slopes.
  • If you are an adventure enthusiast, then, you can try activities like trekking, skiing, and mountaineering (at your own), evening at leisure.

Adventure activities available on extra cost (as per availability):

  • Cable Car Ropeway
  • Amateur Skiing
  • Snow Scooter
  • Zorbing
  • Paragliding
  • River rafting

Option1. (Rohtang Pass)

You can also visit Rohtang Pass – this will be solely at the discretion of the local & , NGT , governing authorities and at extra cost. (Permit Availability)

Option2. (Atal Tunnel)

You can also visit and drive – through the newly built Atal Tunnel, one of the highest Tunnels in the World. This tunnel acts as a gateway to the Lahaul Spiti Valley. You can make a stop at the North-Portal of the tunnel for an amazing photo-shoot. This option willbe at extra cost.

Note: Kindly inform us to add these options at the time of booking.

  • In the evening, return back to Manali town.
  • Overnight stay at Manali.
DAY 5

Manali Local Sightseeing

▼
  • Morning at leisure. Post breakfast proceed for the sightseeing tour of Manali including visit to Vashisht Hot Water Springs and Temple, Manu Temple, Hadimba Temple, Nehru Kund, and Tibetan monastery. Enjoy rope way ride and paragliding activities (at your own). Manali and enjoy your shopping at the Manali mall road.
  • Overnight stay at Manali.
DAY 6

Manali- Delhi (530 Km/ 11-12 hrs.)

▼
  • Wake up in the morning and after having delicious breakfast Check out from the Hotel. Finally say goodbye to Manali and drive to Delhi carrying beautiful memories of the trip. Drop at Delhi Airport /Railway station to catch onward Flights/train.

* Return home with wonderful memories of your tour *
