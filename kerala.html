Highlights of Kerala 5 Nights - 6 Days

DAY 1

Cochin Arrival & City Tour

▼
  • Upon arrival at Cochin Airport/Railway station You will meet Our representative welcomes you and drive to the Cochin Hotel.
  • Check into the hotel/resort and take a rest. Afternoon Visit The Dutch Palace, Jewish Synagogue the oldest European church in India, Santa Cruz Basilica.
  • In theEvening visit Marine Drive and return to hotel overnight at Cochin.
DAY 2

Cochin Munnar (127 Km/4-5 Hrs )

▼
  • After morningbreakfast drive to Munnar, which is called “Nature Lover’s Paradise” and is at 1800mts above sea level.
  • On the way to Munnar visit Cheyappara Waterfalls and Valara Waterfalls. Visit the plush tea gardens, and experience this vast plantation area around the slopes of the hills.
  • In the Evening checkinto the hotel/resort. Overnight at Munnar.
DAY 3

Munnar Sightseeing

▼
  • AfterMorning breakfast proceed for Munnar sightseeing, and visit Mattupetty Dam, Kundala Dam, and Eco Point, another serene and tranquil spot.
  • In the Afternoon visit the Tea Museum.
  • After that get back to the hotel or Overnight at Munnar Hotel.
DAY 4

Munnar – Thekkady (96 Km /2-3 Hrs )

▼
  • Afterbreakfast, check out of the hotel/resort and proceed to Thekkady. It's 02- 03 Hours.
  • Check into the hotel/resort. After rest going to the Sightseen. Have an amazing boat ride in Periyar Lake which flows through the Periyar Tiger Reserve. You will have a chance to watch the wild animals like elephants, Bison, Sambar, Deer etc.
  • At the Evening visit the local spice market for fresh garden spices. And get back to the hotel and Overnight at Thekkady Hotel.
DAY 5

Thekkady - Alappuzha (138 Km/4-5 Hrs )

▼
  • After Morning breakfast, Check out from the hotel and proceed to Alappuzha which has an immense natural beauty, also known as the “Venice of the East.
  • Check in to the Hotel/ Houseboat. Enjoy the backwater of Alleppey, The Shikara boat cruise is advisable for the guests who are staying in a hotel in Alleppey to enjoy the Backwater.
  • Overnight stay at Alleppey.
DAY 6

Cochin Departure (53 Km /1-2Hrs )

▼
  • After Morning ` breakfast proceed to Cochin airport/railway station and depart. Travel back to your home with wonderful memories of an amazing experience in Kerala.
