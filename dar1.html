DAY 2

Excursion to Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir

▼
  • Morning Breakfast - Excursion to Tsomgo Lake 2 Hours’ Drive - Nestled high in the Himalayas at an altitude of 12,310 feet, Tsomgo Lake (also known as Changu Lake) is a breathtaking alpine lake that captivates visitors with its ethereal beauty. Surrounded by towering snow-capped peaks and lush green meadows, the shimmering waters of Tsomgo reflect the changing skies, offering a mesmerizing sight. The lake’s serene environment, particularly during the winter months when it freezes, creates a magical atmosphere that feels straight out of a fairy tale.Just a short distance away, the revered Baba Mandir stands as a testament to the devotion and courage of the legendary Baba Harbhajan Singh, a soldier who became a folk hero.
DAY 3

Gangtok Local Sightseeing

▼
  • Gangtok is a charming hill station and the capital of Sikkim, known for its breathtaking views of the Himalayas, vibrant Tibetan culture, serene monasteries, and lush landscapes. It offers a peaceful blend of natural beauty, spirituality, and modern charm, making it a perfect retreat for nature lovers and cultural enthusiasts alike.
  • This Morning After Breakfast Visit (Tashi View Point, Ganesh Tak, Hanuman Tak) Flower Show, Directorate Of Handicraft & Handloom (Closed On Sunday), Dro-Dul Chorten, Bakthang Falls, Gunjan Monastery.
  • Overnight stay at Gangtok.
DAY 4

Gangtok To Darjeeling Transfer

▼
  • The distance from Gangtok to Darjeeling is approximately 100 kilometers (about 4 to 5 hours by road). The journey offers stunning views of the Himalayas, lush tea gardens, and winding mountain roads. As you travel, you'll pass through quaint villages, terraced fields, and dense forests. The route is a scenic delight, with glimpses of the majestic Kanchenjunga peak, making it an unforgettable experience for nature lovers. The cool climate and serene surroundings only add to the charm of this picturesque road trip.
  • Overnight stay at Darjeeling
