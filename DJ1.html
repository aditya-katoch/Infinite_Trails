Delhi

DAY 1

Arrival Delhi

▼
  • Morning arrival at Delhi Railway/ Airport. Meet and greet by our representative and transfer to hotel (early check in subject to availability & standard check in time is 12 pm). After Fresh up visit to India gate. Evening is free for leisure.
  • Over Night Stay in Hotel .
DAY 2

Delhi City Tour

▼
  • Morning Post breakfast in the hotel. Proceed forward to visit Red Fort, Raj Ghat, Jama Masjid, Humayun’s Tomb, and Qutab Minar.
  • Overnight stay in Delhi.
DAY 3

Transfer To Agra Vist Taj Mahal & Agra Fort ( 221km/3-4hrs.)

▼
  • Morning Post breakfast Check out in the hotel and transfer to Agra in a private ac car and upon arrival proceed further for check-in in the hotel.
  • Later move further to visit the Taj Mahal & Agra Fort. The evening will be in-market to explore.
DAY 4

Agra - Jaipur ( 238/4-5 hrs.)

▼
  • Morning Post breakfast Check out from hotel and transfer to Jaipur in a private vehicle.
  • After check-in and a short break, move forward for local sightseeing of Amer Fort. Enjoy Elephant ride in Amer Fort.
DAY 5

Jaipur Local Sightseeing

▼
  • Morning Post breakfast in the hotel. Proceed for local sightseeing where we shall cover Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jantar-Mantar, and Govind Dev Ji Temple. Also visit Birla temple.
  • In the evening, the driver cum guide will take you to the local market near the Hawa Mahal for Shopping.
DAY 6

Jaipur to Delhi Drop

▼
  • Morning Post breakfastCheck out from hotel and departure to Delhi in the same private vehicle and accordingly drop at Delhi airport / Railway Station.
