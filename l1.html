Details

Enchanting Ladakh with Kargil For 7 nights & 8 days

DAY 1

ARRIVE LEH

▼
  • Arrival at Leh Kushok Bakula Rinpoche Airport and transfer to hotel.
  • Check in and take a rest to acclimatise yourself to the higher Altitude (Note : Early check is subject to Availability as per the standard check-in time 12:00 Noon )
  • Later by the second half proceed for the sightseeing of Leh
  • Shanti Stupa: A beautiful withe Domed Structure that was built by the Japanese Buddhists and a symbol of Universal Brotherhood and peace.
  • Leh Palace: It's a 17th Century of Architecture built by the ruler of the Namgyal Dynasty witness the panoramic view of Leh Town.
  • Stroll at the local market of Leh and enjoy the peaceful Atmosphere.
  • By the evening get back to the hotel and have a delicious Dinner.
  • And overnight stay at Leh.
DAY 2

LEH - ECXURSION TO SHAM VALLEY

▼
  • Wake up to a beautiful morning and have your breakfast and proceed for the day trip to Sham Valley known specially for the scenic views and historical Landmarks.
  • Hall of Fame: A muesum dedicated to the Indian Army and ladakh History.
  • Visit the Spituk Gompa which also has a museum on the premises housing ancient masks, weapons and Thangkas (Buddhist Paintings).
  • Visit Pathar Sahib Gurudwara a Sacred Sikh Shrine and get to know the history behind the Pilgrimage and have a Delicious Langar Prasad.
  • Visit the most interesting path Magnetic hill where vehicles appear to defy the Law of Gravity.
  • Witness the beautiful Sangam of Indus and Zanskar Rivers.
  • And after an Adventurous day get back to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay in Leh.
DAY 3

LEH TO NUBRA VIA KHARDUNG-LA PASS

▼
  • After having a healthy breakfast in the morning, get ready for the ultimate adventure.
  • Drive towards Nubra valley the journey involves Khardungla-La Pass one of the highest motorable Roads in the world.
  • Visit Diskit Monestry which is the oldest and largest monastery of Numbra famous for the Giant Buddha Statue overlooking the Nubra Valley.
  • After that explore the charming villages of Nubra Hunder & Tegar.
  • And enjoy the short Camel ride on Sand Dunes at Hunder Village.
  • Later enjoy camping with a bonfire under the sky full of stars.
  • Overnight stay at the campsite in Nubra Valley.
DAY 4

NUBRA VALLEY TO PANGONG LAKE

▼
  • Wake up in the morning in the beautiful Nubra Valley and have a scrumptious breakfast.
  • Get ready for the most awaited day as you will be visiting Pangong Lake on the Indo-China border.
  • One of the highest altitude saline water lakes, Pangong Tso will greet you with its changing colours from shades of blue to green to red.
  • Spend the rest of the day exploring the surroundings of the Pangong Lake with the view of the Shyok river on one side and the mountains on another side.
  • You can also recreate some of the moments from your favourite Bollywood movies like 3-idiots and Jab Tak Hai Jaan and enjoy the place in Bollywood style.
  • Overnight stay and dinner at a campsite near Pangong Lake.
DAY 5

PANGONG LAKE TO LEH VIA CHANG- LA PASS

▼
  • Wake up in the morning to the beautiful Pangong Lake view and have a delicious breakfast.
  • drive back to Leh via Chang La Pass (17,590 feet). The pass is known for its spectacular views of the Ladakh range
  • Visit the Changla Baba Temple on top, after which the pass got its name and is believed to be the guardian of the people visiting Chang La.
  • Further, visit the Druk Padma School, famed for the movie ‘3 Idiots’ as the site where the last scene was shot.
  • Upon reaching Leh, rest for some time followed by dinner at the hotel.
  • Overnight stay at a hotel in Leh.
DAY 6

LEH –KARGIL VIA ALCHI &LAMAYURU VILLAGE/24OKM 7-8HRS

▼
  • Wake up to a beutiful morning in the mountains.
  • After breakfast drive towards Kargil enqoute Visit Alchi Village.
  • Visit Alchi Monestry one of famous and oldest monestry in Ladakh.
  • Pass through Lamayuru Village visit Lamayuru Monastery, known for its unique lunar-like landscape and spectacular views.
  • Reach Kargil in the evening and Dinner & overnight stay at Kargil.
DAY 7

KARGIL –DRAS-LEH

▼
  • Wake up and have delicious breakfast and pass through Dras, which holds the distinction of being the second coldest inhabited place in the world.
  • Visit Dras War Memorial(Muesum) ,Dedicated to the soldiers who fought in the Kargil war 1999.
  • Now drive back to Leh and enroute visit Mulbekh where you can see Buddha Statues Carved on a Single Rock.
  • Drive thourgh Namika-La & Fatu-La pass the highest of leh & Srinagar.
  • Arrive at Leh and overnight stay at Leh.
DAY 8

DEPARTURE (FLY OUT)

▼
  •  Wake up in the morning and after having delicious breakfast Check out from the Hotel. Finally say goodbye to Leh Ladakh and transfer to airport with beautiful memories of the trip
