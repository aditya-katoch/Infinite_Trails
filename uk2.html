DAY 1

Delhi to Mussoorie

▼
  • On Arrival at Delhi Airport / Railway Station You will be met , our assisted and drive to Mussoorieits approx. 6-7 hours. Reach in Mussoorie check into the hotel. Evening you is free for your leisure time. In the evening escort back to hotel, have dinner and Overnight stay overnight at hotel.
DAY 2

Explore Mussoorie

▼
  • After Morning breakfast proceed to Mussoorie sightseen and Visit Kempty Fallsit’s a famous waterfall located around 15 km from the main town.Explore Mussoorie Lake for boating. And AfternoonHead to Lal Tibba the highest point in Mussoorie for panoramic views andVisit Happy Valley and Tibetan Temple. AtEveningEnjoy the local food at Mall Road or a nearby café and Overnight stay in Hotel .
DAY 3

Mussoorie to Jim Corbett National Park

▼
  • After Morning breakfast, drive from Mussoorie to Jim Corbett National Parkits Approx. 6 hours . On ArrivalCorbett National Park Check in to your hotel/resort In the Evening enjoy the peaceful ambiance of the jungle, or take a nature walk around the resort.And Overnight Stay at hotel/resort near Jim Corbett.
DAY 4

Jim Corbett Safari & Explore

▼
  • Early morning post breakfast , head out for a safari in Jim Corbett National Park. The morning safari gives you a chance to spot more wildlife as the animals are more active.There are several zones in the park like Dhikala, Bijrani, Jhirna, and Durga Devi for safari.And after that Visit Garjiya Devi Temple or enjoy another round of safari in the evening (If possible).And Overnight Stay at hotel/ resort near Jim Corbett.
DAY 5

Jim Corbett to Nainital

▼
  • Morning Post breakfast, drive from Jim Corbett to Nainital.its approx4-5 hours.On Arrival toNainital check in to your hotel. Freshen up and rest.And After that VisitNaini Lake and enjoy a boat ride Or Naina Devi Temple . And At the Evening Explore the Mall Road and shop for local itemsandOvernight Stay in Nainital Hotel.
DAY 6

Explore Nainital and Return to Delhi

▼
  • Morning post breakfast proceed to Snow View Point by cable car. In afternoon Head to Naina Peak (highest point in Nainital) for breathtaking views of the Himalayas.And Visit the Zoo and The Governor's House.In the Evening Begin your journey back to Delhi Its approx. 7-8 hours drive to Delhi.
