Trip Highlights

  • Scenic drive from Chandigarh to Shimla, passing through picturesque landscapes.
  • Exploration of Kufri, known for its stunning views and adventure activities.
  • Visit to Christ Church and Jakhu Temple, offering a serene spiritual experience.
  • Experience the bustling Mall Road, The Ridge, and Lakkar Bazaar for shopping and leisure.
  • Sightseeing in Kullu Valley, famous for its natural beauty and adventure activities.
  • A full-day excursion to Solang Valley, offering skiing, paragliding, zorbing, and ATV rides.
  • Visit to Atal Tunnel & Sissu Lake, a breathtaking experience amidst snow-covered peaks.
  • Manali City Tour, covering Hadimba Temple, Tibetan Monastery, Vashisht Hot Springs, and Club House.
  • Leisure time at Manali Mall Road and Van Vihar, perfect for relaxation and shopping.
  • Return journey to Chandigarh, with memories of the breathtaking Himachal landscapes.
DAY 1

Chandigarh- Shimla (120 Km/3-4hrs)

▼
  • On arrival at Chandigarh Airport/ Railway Station you will be met, assisted and drive to Shimla.It will take around 3-4hrs.to reach Shimla by surface from Chandigarh.
  • Overnight stay at Shimla Hotel.
DAY 2

Shimla-Kufri& City Tour

▼
  • Morning visit to Kufri&Himalayan Zoo followed by The Christ Church, Jakhu Temple (Via rope Way)a famous temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman. It's located at the highest point in Shimla, offering breathtaking views.The Gaiety Theatre, Scandal Point &LakkarBazzar,The Mall Road and The Ridge.
  • Visit to Indian Institute of Advanced Study (formerly known as Vice Regal Lodge, closed on Monday).The Indian Institute of Advanced Study (IIAS) in Shimla is a premier research institution dedicated to promoting advanced study and research in the fields of humanities, social sciences, and natural sciences.
  • Overnight stay at Shimla Hotel.
DAY 3

Shimla - Manali Via Kullu Valley (270 Km/8-9 hrs.)

▼
  • Morning drive from Shimla to Manali. It’s an 8–9-hour long journey.
  • Morning drive from Shimla to Manali. It’s an 8–9-hour long journey.
  • Manali is a popular hill station located in the state of Himachal Pradesh, India. Situated at an altitude of 2,050 meters (6,726 feet) above sea level, Manali is nestled in the Beas River Valley and is surrounded by snow-capped peaks, lush green valleys, and pine forests.
  • Overnight stay at Manali Hotel.
DAY 4

Manali: SolangValley, Atal Tunnel &Sisu Lake

▼
  • Post breakfast proceed for day excursion toSolang Valley- A splendid valley between Solang village and Beas Kund which offers the view of glaciers and snowcapped mountains and peaks which has fine ski slopes.
  • Solang Valley offers thrilling adventure activities like skiing, paragliding, zorbing, and snowboarding in winter, and trekking, ATV riding, and mountain biking in summer. It’s a popular destination for adrenaline seekers year-round.
  • Later visit to Atal Tunnel &Sissulake.(The Atal Tunnel is a 9.02 km long, all-weather tunnel connecting Manali to Lahaul-Spiti Valley, built beneath the Rohtang Pass in Himachal Pradesh.)
  • Overnight stay at the Manali hotel.
DAY 5

Manali City Tour

▼
  • Post breakfast proceed for the sightseeing tour of Manali including visit to Vashisht Hot Water Springs and Temple,(Vashisht Village is known for its natural hot springs, which are believed to have medicinal properties. These baths are great for a relaxing dip after a long day of exploring), Tibetan monastery, Clube House, van Vihar, and Manali Mall Road.
  • HadimbaTemple :A unique, ancient wooden temple dedicated to Hadimba Devi, the wife of Bhima from the Mahabharata.
  • Evening is at leisure. Overnight stay at theManali hotel.
DAY 6

Manali- Chandigarh (310 Km/7-8hrs.)

▼
  • After breakfast Check out from the Hotel and drive back to Chandigarh.Finally say goodbye to Manali and drive to Chandigarh carrying beautiful memories of the trip. Drop at Chandigarh Airport /Railway station to catch onward Flights/train.

* Return home with wonderful memories of your tour *
