Srinagar

DAY 1

Arrival Srinagar

▼
  • After arrival at Srinagar, meet and greet by our representative and transfer to Hotel in Srinagar. Check into Hotel, after freshen up later enjoy Shikara Ride on the famous Dal Lake to enjoy the beauty that God has graced this city, which is often compared to Venice, Evening free for leisure.
  • Dinner & Overnight stay at Houseboat in Srinagar.
DAY 2

Excursion to Gulmarg (approx. 50 kms one way)

▼
  • After Breakfast, transfer to Gulmarg (8700 fti.e., 2652 mts), Discovered by the Kashmiri romantic poet in the 16th century who was inspired with its grassy slopes covered with wild flowers. Later visit Gondola Cable Car offers rides to the upland meadows of Kongdori and beyond to the top of Apharwat range to a height of 14000 ft for viewing the Himalayan Peaks in summer and for downhill skiing in winter. One can have Gondola ride (cable car directly payable) from Gulmarg to Khalinmarg.
  • At evening, return back to Srinagar.
  • Note: The vehicle will drop and pick you up at Gulmarg car stand, for local convenience one has to rely on Poneys and ATV rides payable directly to explore other places in Gulmarg.
DAY 3

Srinagar - Pahalgam (90Km/2-3 hrs.)

▼
  • This day after breakfast checkout from hotel and transfer to Pahalgam, which is the most famous place for Indian Film Industry shoot. Arrive in Pahalgam, Check-in at the hotel.
  • Later visit Betab Valley, Chandanwari& Aru Valley, The most Scenic locations of Kashmir(by local taxis Payable directly).
  • You may also visit Baisaran Valley on poneys (Payable directly). In the evening, return to hotel.
  • Overnight stay at Pahalgam hotel.
  • Note: To visit Betab Valley, Chandanwari& Aru Valley you have to hire local taxis Payable directly.
DAY 4

Pahalgam-Srinagar & Local Sightseeing

▼
  • After breakfast, transfer to Srinagar, on arrival in Srinagar visit famous Nishat Garden known as ‘Garden of pleasure' built in 16th century (1633) by Mughal King Asif Khan. Then visiting Shalimar Garden known as ‘Garden of love' built in 16th century (1619) by Mughal King Jehangir for his beloved wife Noor Jehan. Also visit Shankaracharyatemple situated on top of a hill where you can have a view of whole Srinagar City. Enjoy shopping at Kashmiri craft & art stores.
  • Dinner & Overnight Stay at Hotel in Srinagar.
DAY 5

Departure

▼
  • After breakfast, check out from Hotel and transfer to Srinagar Airportto connect to onwards destination with unforgettable memories of Kashmir.

* Return home with wonderful memories of your tour *
