Chardham Yatra 11 Nights &12 Days Ex-Delhi “Char Dham Yatra”

DAY 1

Delhi- Haridwar (220 Km/4-5 Hrs.)

▼
  • Arrival at Delhi railway Station and drive to Haridwar via Ghaziabad Meerut Roorkee.
  • On arrival check in to hotel and after fresh up visit to Hari Ki Pauri,Bharat Mata Mandir Chandi Devi Temple (by ropeway) then visit to Ganga aarti.
  • Later back to hotel for overnight stay.
DAY 2

Haridwar to Barkot Via Mussoorie (190 Km/8-9 Hrs)

▼
  • After breakfast checkout from Haridwar and drive to Barkot. Enroute you can halt for some time at Mussoorie.
  • Later proceed to Barkot and transfer to your hotel, have dinner and stay overnight at the hotel.
DAY 3

Barkot- Janki Chatti & Janki Chatti to Yamunotri trek-Return Barkot

▼
  • After Breakfast, Drive till Janki Chatti and then trek / palki / horse (your own expenses) for 06 km to reach Yamunotri.
  • Visit the temple of Goddess Yamuna which is situated on the banks of the river. There is a hot water spring adjacent to this temple. The pilgrims take a holy dip this spring before the darshan of the temple. You can also cook rice in these hot waters. After darshan return back to Janki Chatti and then to Bakrot.
DAY 4

Bakrot- Uttarkashi (80 Km/ 4 Hrs)

▼
  • After breakfast, drive to Uttarkashi. You can visit the famous Vishwanath Temple.
  • Situated on the banks of River Bhagirathi is Uttarkashi, an important pilgrim center which is often equated with Kashi (Varanasi). It is situated at an elevation of 1150 meters.
DAY 5

Uttarkashi - Gangotri- Uttarkashi (100 Km/4 Hrs One Way)

▼
  • After breakfast, we proceed to Harsil. This is one of the most beautiful valleys in Uttarakhand. On the way You can halt at Gangnani which again has hot water springs. You can take a dip here. Then drive to Gangotri (22 kms/1 hr). Over here devotees take a holy dip in the most Holy River of Hindus- The Ganges. It is also called Bhagirathi from where it originates.
  • You can then perform pooja and enjoy the picturesque surroundings. In the evening returned back to Uttarkashi.
DAY 6

Uttarkashi- Guptkashi (193 kms / 07-8 hrs.)

▼
  • After breakfast, checkout from the hotel and drive to Guptkashi. You can explore Dhari Devi Temple, Rudraprayag on the way. Afternoon you will reach Guptkashi. then evening local sightseeing famous Hindu temple Vishwanath, then evening have dinner and overnight Stay.
DAY 7

Guptkashi – Sonpryag (Gauri Kund) - Kedarnath

▼
  • Post your early breakfast drive till Sonpryag (Gauri Kund) and start your 18 Km trek to Kedarnath.
  • After darshan and Pooja at Kedarnath Temple walk back to hotel (Own Arrangement).
  • NOTE: If you will go Kedarnath by helicopter then same day return to Guptkashi/ Phata.

DAY 8

Trek down to Gauri Kund (18 Km)- Guptkashi (23 Km/1 Hr.)

▼
  • Wake up early in the morning and visit Morning aarti after completing the puja process, trek down to Gauri Kund. Here will meet the driver and drive back to Guptkashi.
  • Overnight stay at hotel.
DAY 9

Guptkashi –Badrinath (Joshimath) (200 Km/9-10 hrs.)

▼
  • After an early breakfast, we proceed to Badrinath Dham via Joshimath, Badrinath is one of the most famous pilgrim centres for Hinduism.
  • This temple is dedicated to lord Vishnu. On arrival check in, process some rest and visit evening aarti and after holy darshan go back to the hotel to have dinner overnight in Badrinath or in Joshimath.
DAY 10

Badrinath (Joshimath) - Rudraprayag (154 Km/ 5-6 Hrs)

▼
  • Post breakfast check out from hotel and drive to Rudraprayag enroute visiting Brahma kapal, Charan paduka, Narad kund, Tapt Kund, Mata murti Temple (According to Time) and later in evening drive to Rudraprayag.
  • On arrival check in to hotel for overnight stay.
DAY 11

Rudraprayag to Rishikesh (140 Km/5-6 hrs.)

▼
  • After breakfast checkout from Rudraprayag and drive to Rishikesh. On arrival check in to hotel. After some rest and if time allows You can do adventure activities like river rafting, River’s bungee, Rock Climbing etc (on direct payment) then go back to the hotel had dinner overnight.
DAY 12

Rishikesh- Delhi (240 Km/ 4-5 hrs.)

▼
  • After breakfast checkout from Rishikesh and drive to Delhi. Then your own journey towards the home with happy memories of the tour.
