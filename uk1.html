Rishikesh – Jim Corbett – Mussoorie 05 Night 06 Days

DAY 1

Delhi to Rishikesh

▼
  • On Arrival at Delhi Airport / Railway Station You will be met , our assisted and drive to Rishikesh its approx. 5-6 hours.And the AfternoonArrive in Rishikesh and check-in to your hotel.In the Evening Visit the famous Triveni Ghat for the Ganga Aarti. The Aarti here is mesmerizing and will give you a peaceful start to your journey.Overnight Stay in Rishikesh Hotel .
DAY 2

Explore Rishikesh

▼
  • Morning post breakfast, proceeds for sightseen tour of Rishikesh and visit the Laxman Jhulaand Ram Jhula for their spiritual significance and scenic beauty.
  • You can also try some adventure activities like river rafting , trekking around the area.
  • In the afternoon Visit the Neelkanth Mahadev Temple or explore the Rajaji National Park for nature walks and wildlife.In evening Relax by the river, enjoy the peaceful vibe of the town, and you can also indulge in some yoga or meditation.Overnight Stay in Rishikesh.
DAY 3

Rishikesh to Jim Corbett

▼
  • Morning post breakfast , drive to Jim Corbett National Parkits approx. 5-6 hours.By AfternoonCheck-in at your hotel or resort near Corbett. Rest for a while and In Evening If time permits, enjoy a jungle safari at the park (Book in advance). You might spot tigers, elephants, and various wildlife species.And Overnight Stay at a resort near Jim Corbett.
DAY 4

Jim Corbett Safari & Explore

▼
  • Early morning post breakfast , head out for a safari in Jim Corbett National Park. The morning safari gives you a chance to spot more wildlife as the animals are more active. InAfternoon Return to the hotel to relax . Afterward, explore the surroundings . Evening: Visit Garjiya Devi Temple or enjoy another round of safari in the evening (If possible).And Overnight Stay at a resort near Jim Corbett.
DAY 5

Jim Corbett to Mussoorie

▼
  • Morning Post breakfast, drive from Jim Corbett to Mussoorie.its approx. 6-7 hours.On Arrival to Mussoorie check in to your hotel. Freshen up and rest.And After that Visit the famous Mall Road for shopping and scenic views. You can also visit Gun Hillfor a panoramic view of the surrounding mountains andOvernight Stay in Mussoorie Hotel.
DAY 6

Explore Mussoorie and Return to Delhi

▼
  • Morning post breakfast proceed to Kempty Falls(A beautiful waterfall located just outside the town).And Explore Mussoorie Lake, Bhatta Falls, and the nearby viewpoints.In the Evening Begin your journey back to Delhi Its approx. 6-7 hours drive to Delhi.
