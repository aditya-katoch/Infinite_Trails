DAY 1

Arival Goa

▼
  • Arrival Goa and check in to hotel. Enjoy the first day of your Goa trip leisurely, go out exploring the surroundings or the beaches nearby while having a great time with your loved ones.
  • Overnight Stay at hotel.
DAY 2

North Goa Sightseeing Tour

▼
  • Post breakfast get ready for sightseeing of North Goa Includes: Calangute beach, Baga Beach, Fort Aguada, Salegaon Church, Anjuna Beach & Vagator.
  • Calangute Beach, often dubbed the "Queen of Beaches," is one of the most popular and bustling beaches in Goa, located in North Goa. It’s known for its lively atmosphere, golden sands, and vibrant beach culture.
  • Fort Aguada is one of Goa’s most iconic landmarks, rich in history and offering spectacular views of the Arabian Sea. Located near Sinquerim Beach in North Goa, this 17th-century Portuguese fort is a must-visit for history buffs and sightseeing enthusiasts.
  • Overnight Stay at hotel.
DAY 3

South Goa Sightseeing Tour

▼
  • Post breakfast get ready for sightseeing of South Goa Includes: Panjim City, Panjim Church, Panjim Shopping, Miramar Beach, Dona Paula, Old Goa Churches & Museum.
  • Panjim, the capital city of Goa, is home to the beautiful Church of Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception, commonly known as Panjim Church. It’s one of the most famous and picturesque churches in Goa, showcasing stunning Portuguese colonial architecture and a rich history.
  • Old Goa, often referred to as “Rome of the East,” is a historic town in Goa known for its magnificent churches and museums.
DAY 4

Departure

▼
