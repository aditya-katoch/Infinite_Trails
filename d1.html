Delhi

DAY 1

Delhi - Haridwar (220 Km Drive - 5 Hours)

▼
  • Arrival at Delhi railway Station and drive to Haridwar via Ghaziabad Meerut Roorkee.
  • On arrival check in to hotel and after fresh up visit to Hari Ki Pauri,Bharat Mata Mandir Chandi Devi Temple (by ropeway) Daksha Mahadev Temple then visit to Ganga aarti.
  • Later back to hotel for overnight stay.
DAY 2

Haridwar - Guptkashi (210 KM DRIVE / 07-08 HRS)

▼
  • After breakfast checkout from Haridwar and drive to Guptkashi /Phata.
  • On arrival check in into the hotel, rest of the day if your spirit allows visit famous temple Vishwanath temple and Manikaran Kund and then back to hotel for overnight stay.
DAY 3

Guptkashi – Kedarnath (DRIVE: 35 Km / 16 Km Trek )

▼
  • Post your early breakfast drive till Gauri Kund and start your 18 Km trek to Kedarnath.
  • After darshan and Pooja at Kedarnath Temple walk back to hotel (Own Arrangement).
  • NOTE: If you will go Kedarnath by helicopter then same day return to Guptkashi/ Phata.
  • By Helicopter: In the morning our driver will pick you up and transfer you to the helipad (Phata, Guptkashi, Sirsi, etc.) *(Helicopter ticket is not included in this package).
  • By Trek: In the morning our driver will drop you at Sonprayag then you have to take a local jeep to Gaurikund and then start your trek to Kedarnath ji. You can also take a horse, doli, pittu, or palanquin from Gaurikund at your own cost, after Kedarnath Ji.
DAY 4

Kedarnath - Guptkashi (TREK: 16 Km / DRIVE: 35 Km)

▼
  • Wake up early in the morning and visit Morning aarti after completing the puja process, trek down to Gauri Kund. Here will meet the driver and drive back to Guptkashi.Overnight stay at hotel.
DAY 5

Guptkashi – Ukhimath- Joshimath (158 Km/6-7 hrs.)

▼
  • After breakfast checkout from Guptkashi and drive to Joshimath Enroute visiting Ukhimath.
  • Evening check in to hotel.
DAY 6

Joshimath - Badrinath (45 Km/2 Hrs.) - Rudraprayag

▼
  • Wake up early morning and visit morning aarti and have breakfast and checkout from hotel and drive to Badrinath. You can cover the local places like, Brahma kapal, Charan paduka, Narad Kund, Tapt Kund, Mata murti Temple (According to Time) and later in evening drive to Rudraprayag.
  • On arrival check in to hotel for overnight stay.
DAY 7

Rudraprayag to Haridwar (165 Km/4 hrs.)

▼
  • After breakfast, checkout from Rudraprayag and drive to Rishikesh. On arrival visit to Ram Jhula, Laxman Jhula, 13 male Temple.
  • Later check in to hotel for overnight stay.
DAY 8

Haridwar to Delhi (250 km/7-8 hrs.)

▼

Morning post breakfast check out from hotel and drive back to Delhi.
