Our Story

Rooted in the heart of Himachal Pradesh, we are a community driven by passion, expertise, and a love for travel.

About Us

At Infinite Trails Private Limited, we are proud to be founded by a team of seasoned tourism professionals with years of hands-on experience in the travel industry. Before starting this community-driven venture, our founders held roles in some of the most reputed travel companies, including Cox & Kings and Sita Travels. These experiences gave them deep industry insights, customer service expertise, and a global perspective — all of which now shape the way we serve our clients.

Rooted in the enchanting land of Gods — Himachal Pradesh — all our founders are alumni of Himachal Pradesh University, where they earned a Master’s in Tourism Administration (MTA). This university is known for producing tourism professionals who have gone on to lead at top levels in the travel and hospitality sector.

True explorers at heart, our founding team has travelled extensively — trekking majestic peaks, discovering hidden gems, and building valuable connections across the travel community. These journeys and professional milestones laid the foundation for Infinite Trails, a company now recognized and appreciated by leading tourism networks across India.

We are proud of the rapid growth and recognition we’ve achieved — thanks to our clear vision, customer-first approach, and highly structured administration.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a trustworthy and professional travel community that offers reliable, personalized, and affordable travel services. We aim to set an example for aspiring tourism professionals and help raise the standard of service in the industry through passion, expertise, and innovation.
