Thrill Leh-Ladakh with Pangong Lake 5Night 6Days Ex-Leh

DAY 1

ARRIVE LEH

▼
  • Arrival Leh Kushok Bakula Rinpoche Airport and transfer to hotel.
  • Upon arrival you will met by our representative and transfer to Hotel for Check in (early Check in is subject to availability as standard check in time is 1200 hrs).
  • Arrival in Leh: Fly into Leh's Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport. Enjoy the beautiful mountain views as you land.
  • Acclimatization: Leh is at a high altitude, so it's essential to rest and acclimatize to avoid altitude sickness.
  • Explore Leh Market: If you feel up to it, take a light stroll through the local market. You can also visit the Shanti Stupa for a panoramic view of Leh.

DAY 2

Leh City tour Shayam valley

▼
  • After morning breakfast visit to Hall of Fame.
  • Gurudwara Patthar Sahib: Dedicated to Guru Nanak Dev Ji and houses a scared boulder which is believed to have imprints of the guru.
  • Magnetic Hill. There is a mysterious magnetic force emanating from the hill that pulls stationary vehicles within its range and makes them move uphill.
  • Sindhu Ghat: Visit the banks of the Indus River for some serene moments.

DAY 3

Leh to Pangong Lake (Approx. 4-5 hours drive)

▼
  • Morning After breakfastdrive to Pangong LakeStart early for a journey through the rugged terrain of Leh, passing through the Chang La Pass (17,590 feet). The drive to Pangong is stunning with scenic views of the mountains and valleys.
  • Thiksey Monastery: This is one of the most iconic monasteries in Ladakh, known for its resemblance to the Potala Palace in Lhasa, Tibet.
  • Hemis Monastery: A must-see, it's one of the largest and most famous monasteries in Ladakh.
  • Tso Moriri: High grassland lake.
  • Pangong Lake: Once you reach Pangong, the sight of the shimmering blue water surrounded by barren mountains will take your breath away. Enjoy some time around the lake, click pictures, and absorb the beauty of the place.
  • Overnight stay at Pangong Lake.

Nubra Valley to Leh

▼
  • Drive back to Leh: After breakfast, drive back to Leh via the same route through Khardung La.
  • Explore Nubra Valley: Visit Diskit Monastery and enjoy a visit to the Hunder Sand Dunes, where you can ride a double-humped Bactrian camel.
  • Khardung La Pass(18,380 feet), one of the highest motorable passes in the world.
